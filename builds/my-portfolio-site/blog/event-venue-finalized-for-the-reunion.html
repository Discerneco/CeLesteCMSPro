Event Venue Finalized for the Reunion

By Admin • August 25, 2025

**We** are thrilled to announce that after an extensive search and numerous site visits, we have secured the perfect venue for our upcoming 26-year reunion in Belo Horizonte. The event will be held at the beautiful Centro de Eventos do Minas, which offers ample space for all attendees, excellent accommodations, and a central location that is easily accessible for both local and out-of-town participants.

The venue features a spacious main hall for our primary gathering, separate rooms for smaller group activities, and a stunning outdoor area for the welcome reception.
